How Did the Show Come to Be?

Before SpongeBob existed, Stephen Hillenburg was a marine biologist with a passion for ocean conservation. His educational comic, "The Intertidal Zone", featured anthropomorphic sea creatures designed to teach about underwater ecosystems. Inspired to blend education with humor, he ventured into animation.
